SAPL Mobile Apps

Moble Apps

The mySAPL app works on Android and Apple devices. The phone app allows you to do the following:


  • Browse the catalog
  • Check the status of your account
  • Place holds on library materials, such as manga books, music CDs and DVDs
  • See a map of all the SAPL libraries.

If you have an IPad, you’ll be able to use all of the app’s features including:


  • Pay fines
  • Re-new items.
