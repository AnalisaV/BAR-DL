Overdrive

Most people use the Overdrive app to download and borrow e-books, but the app offers more electronic resources that users are unaware of.

In addition to e-books, you can download audiobooks, magazines and steam video from the library. Just some of the magazine titles include Seventeen, the Smithsonian, and Car & Driver.

No need to worry about fines and when you have to return your electronic check-outs. The downloads will expire once the 21 day lending period is over.
