tutor.com

One of the most popular services the library has is Tutor.com. The service is an online interactive program where teens can ask a live tutor for help. The tutors are certified subject specialist, and are screened, so it is a safe online environment. Many of the specialist are teachers. Subjects cover math, i.e. geometry, algebra, science, English, and social studies.
