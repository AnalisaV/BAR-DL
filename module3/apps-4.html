Freegal

If you love to download music and want to get it for free, the SAPL library’s music service is Freegal. You can download the Freegal app too. The service stands for “free and legal” and the fees are underwritten by the library. There are no ads on Freegal.

There are some key guidelines to note:

  • the download limit is five songs per week.
  • Freegal resets on Mondays, so you can turn to the app and get more songs.
  • the primary source for Freegal is the Sony catalog, and the database updates daily.
  • the Sony catalog is vast with a great variety of music. The catalog holds tens of thousands of albums and millions of songs. The catalog has over 28,000 music labels.
