The San Antonio Public Library’s (SAPL) mission statement is to “change lives through the transformative power of information, imagination, and ideas.” With its’ many educational and cultural programs, library facilities, and informational resources; the SAPL library has served residents and beyond for more than 110 years.

This module will cover standard guidelines for the public library and proceed to more in depth about the resources available in the SAPL.
