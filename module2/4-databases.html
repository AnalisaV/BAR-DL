Databases

Databases can help you find online scholarly journals, articles, magazines, and many more!
Each database is different and has so much to offer! Some examples of different databases are listed below.

Explora
For grades from 6-12 and has the ability to search
multiple databases for a great variety of subjects, from literature to current issues in the news.

MAS Ultra-School Edition
Holds full text articles from over 700 high school magazines.
The database includes an image collection consisting of over 1.3 million photos, maps, flags and more.
