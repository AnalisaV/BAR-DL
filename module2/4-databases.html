Captions

In the catalog you can find popular novels, music CD's, manga comics, books, and many more resources for school work. The catalog provides information on where you can find specific materials in the library. Databases can help you find online scholarly journal articles, magazines, and many more! Each database is different and has so much to offer! You can find databases on the library's website in the "Teen Services" section, and selecting "Research & Help". Two specific high school databases are Explora and MAS Ultra-School Edition. Explora is for grades 6 through 12 and is a great source for current events. MAS Ultra-School Edition Database offers downloadable magazine articles and images on geography and culture.

Catalog

Need to find an item in the library?
Then the catalog is your starting place!
In the catalog you can search for books, music, movies, and more!



Databases

There are databases for various types of online materials (newspaper articles, journals, periodicals, etc.)!



Locating the Databases:
 1) Go to the library’s website: www.mysapl.org

2) Click on “Services”

3) Under the heading “For…” click on “Teen Services”

4) Once on Teen Services, click on “Research & Help”



Explora Database
Has the ability to search multiple databases for a great variety of subjects, from literature to current issues in the news.

MAS Ultra-School Edition Database
Holds full text articles from over 700 high school magazines.
The database includes an image collection consisting of over 1.3 million photos, maps, flags and more.
