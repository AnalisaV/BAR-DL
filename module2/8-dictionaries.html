Captions

There are traditional dictionaries that allow you to look up words and you can check out the books too. You can also download e-dictionaries from our website. There are various types of dictionaries, visual, foreign language bilingual, and subject dictionaries. Just like dictionaries, the library has a large selection of thesauri. If you have to write a research paper, the thesaurus will help you find just the right word for your paper. Thesauri books contain synonyms and antonyms. The library carries several varieties of encyclopedias. Encyclopedias are good for finding information quickly. The books contain facts about all different subjects.
