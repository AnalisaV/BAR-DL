The following websites are recommended to accompany the resources discussed in the module. Tutor.com is an 24/7 online tutoring service that assists you with different subjects pertaining to your research. Merriam-Webster Dictionar (http://www.merriam-webster.com) has quizzes and fun facts about words. A free version is also available. Bartleby.com allows you to search for specific quotes from speeches, books, articles, etc. at no cost. Thesaurus.com is a site that allows you to search for specific words and includes a pronunciation tool.

Hover your mouse over each source to see what content each one provides.
